Pyaari Friend ke liye...

Dear Best Friend, Zindagi mein kuch log aise hote hain
jo bina kisi shor ke
hamari duniya ko behtar bana dete hain.
Tum un logon mein se ho
jo har baat samajhne ki koshish karti ho,
jo har situation mein apni positivity se
cheezon ko asaan bana deti ho.
Shukriya hamesha support karne ke liye,
bina judge kiye sun’ne ke liye,
aur meri zindagi ka ek acha hissa banne ke liye.
Chahe zindagi jahan bhi le jaye,
tum hamesha meri best friends mein se ek rahogi.
! 🌸
